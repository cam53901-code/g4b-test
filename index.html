Our Story

From Italy to Hurontario Street

Gabriele Photography was established in 1989 by John Gabriele, inspired by his uncle's photography business in Italy. What started as a small film shop has become Collingwood's hub for photographers of all levels.

We're not a big box store. We're the place where John remembers your camera, Matt finds that impossible battery, and where three generations of families have bought their first camera.

Whether you're shooting on a brand new mirrorless or a Rolleiflex from 1955, you'll get honest advice – not a sales pitch.
